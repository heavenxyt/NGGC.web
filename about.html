Our community started as a PUBG Mobile clan in 2018, founded by Davil and HeavenXR. In 2019, we rebranded as Next Gen. By 2021, we expanded to Discord, forming the Next Gen Gaming Community (NGGC.Discord).

In December 2021, we hosted our first official event, a New State Mobile Tournament.

We have since organized multiple gaming events, including Valorant tournaments, supported by our first sponsor, Game Pocher YT.
